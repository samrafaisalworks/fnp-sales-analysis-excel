FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel Project)
 Project Overview

The FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard developed to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and seasonal trends for Ferns N Petals (FNP).

The project converts raw sales data into meaningful business insights using Excel's advanced analytical features, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions related to marketing, inventory management, customer engagement, and operational efficiency.

 Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are to:

Analyze overall business performance.
Monitor sales and revenue trends.
Identify high-performing occasions and product categories.
Understand customer purchasing behavior.
Evaluate monthly and hourly sales patterns.
Identify top-performing cities.
Measure operational performance through delivery time.
Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

Data Cleaning Process

Before creating the dashboard, the dataset was cleaned to ensure accurate reporting.

The following data cleaning tasks were performed:

Removed duplicate records.
Checked and handled missing values.
Corrected inconsistent text values.
Standardized date formats.
Verified numerical data types.
Cleaned category names.
Organized raw data into structured tables.
Validated data consistency before analysis.

A clean dataset ensures reliable business insights and accurate dashboard calculations.

Business Insights
1. Revenue by Occasion

The dashboard shows that sales are highly influenced by special occasions.

Key Findings
Anniversary generated the highest revenue.
Raksha Bandhan also performed exceptionally well.
Holi recorded strong sales.
Valentine's Day and Diwali generated comparatively lower revenue.
Customer purchasing behavior is event-driven.
Business Recommendation
Increase promotional campaigns before Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan.
Launch early marketing campaigns for lower-performing occasions.
Offer bundled gift packages during festive seasons.
2. Revenue by Category

Revenue distribution across product categories indicates customer preferences.

Key Findings
Soft Toys generated the highest revenue.
Sweets ranked second.
Colors and Raksha Bandhan gifts contributed moderate sales.
Mugs generated the lowest revenue.
Business Recommendation
Increase inventory for Soft Toys.
Promote low-performing categories through discounts.
Introduce combo offers to improve category sales.
3. Revenue by Order Hour

Customer orders fluctuate throughout the day.

Key Findings
Revenue peaks during evening and late-night hours.
Morning sales remain comparatively lower.
Customer purchasing activity is not evenly distributed.
Business Recommendation
Schedule advertisements during peak purchasing hours.
Optimize staffing during busy periods.
Plan faster delivery operations during high-demand hours.
4. Revenue by Month

Monthly sales reveal clear seasonal trends.

Key Findings
February generated the highest revenue.
December also recorded strong sales.
April experienced the lowest revenue.
August showed relatively weaker performance.
Business Recommendation
Prepare inventory before high-demand months.
Launch promotional campaigns during slow months.
Forecast seasonal demand for better stock management.
5. Top Cities by Orders

The dashboard identifies cities generating the highest number of orders.

Key Findings
Kavali recorded the highest order volume.
Imphal and Dhanbad also performed well.
Other cities maintained consistent order activity.
Business Recommendation
Increase marketing investment in high-performing cities.
Expand logistics in regions with increasing demand.
Study customer behavior in top cities to replicate success elsewhere.
6. Top Products by Revenue

The dashboard highlights the highest revenue-generating products.

Key Findings
Dharbhanga generated the highest revenue.
Sirsa also performed strongly.
Ballia, Bally, and Bareilly maintained moderate sales.
Business Recommendation
Prioritize inventory for top-selling products.
Promote these products during festive seasons.
Bundle best-selling products with lower-performing items.
7. Customer Spending Analysis

Average customer spending is:

Rs. 3,520.98

Business Insight

Customers are willing to spend a relatively high amount per order, indicating a premium gifting market.

Recommendation
Introduce premium gift bundles.
Offer loyalty rewards for repeat customers.
Upsell complementary products to increase basket size.
8. Delivery Performance

Average delivery time is:

5.53 Days

Business Insight

Delivery performance is acceptable but can be improved.

Recommendation
Optimize delivery routes.
Improve warehouse operations.
Reduce delivery time during peak seasons.

 Overall Business Findings

The dashboard reveals several important business patterns:

Revenue is highly occasion-driven.
Soft Toys are the most profitable category.
Evening hours generate the highest sales.
February and December are peak sales months.
Certain cities contribute significantly more orders.
Customer spending is relatively high.
Delivery performance has room for improvement.

These insights enable management to make informed business decisions regarding marketing, inventory planning, customer engagement, and logistics.
